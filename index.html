Population at Risk: Fire Hazard Zones in Los Angeles County

This interactive dashboard explores how population across Los Angeles County overlaps with wildfire hazard zones. Using 2020 U.S. Census estimates and CAL FIRE's 2023 Fire Hazard Severity Zone data, it identifies where people are most at risk based on hazard classification.

Explore the interactive map and charts below to see how many people live in each zone—from low to very high fire risk— and which neighborhoods may require the most attention for emergency preparedness and public safety efforts.
